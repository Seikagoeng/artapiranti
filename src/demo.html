SHOPPING BY PLAYING

TingTong is a huge B2C e-commerce platform which includes gaming experience. The content cooperators are including Alfamart which has over 6000 mini shops in Indonesia, Healthy brand Nutrifood, Superstar Fashion brand TokoWOW, etc.

LINK

Link is an integrated app for news subscriptions, agricultural material products price inquiry and forecasting.

HAWA

Hawa is an application to help girls tracking their Menstrual Cycle, getting useful knowledges about Women Health and communicating the topics around Women.

MODOSKY

This is a private customizable website for Hallo Pushing Company. Hallo Pushing Company is a Host bucketing, they use Modosky to help their Hosts to show themselves to all publish users and Agency could manage their Host by this platform too.

SHARE SPACE

Share Space is a C2C space sharing platform. Share Space allows anyone to display any space on this platform and rent it out to any user who is registered on the platform. In addition to space rental services, the platform also allows the rental side to provide additional site peripheral services. Typical venue types are meeting rooms, coffee & bar, wedding venues and more.

AGRI COMMERCE

This is a B2B agriculture e-commerce application which focus on how to solve the problem of asymmetric information between large supermarkets in the city and farmers, so that farmers and large supermarkets and other enterprises can become both buyers and sellers. This application is an Internet+ project which is determined to provide farmers with greater revenue space.

HALAT

Halat is an e-commerce app which sells Muslim clothes and all the staffs which they use in the normal life.
